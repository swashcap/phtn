Section 5.4 Font Sizes

Sizes are based on the Golden Ratio starting at 100% and 81.25%:

1.618 (25/32)  1.315  (21/24)
2.618 (42/48)  2.127  (34/40)
4.236 (68/72)  3.442  (55/64)
